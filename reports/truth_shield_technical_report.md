## 2. Neural Architecture (The Brain)
Our model utilizes a "Fusion-First" approach to verify news from multiple dimensions:

### 2.1 Multilingual Transformer Core
- **Backbone:** `XLM-RoBERTa-base`
- **Capability:** Processed text in English, Hindi, Urdu, Bengali, and 96+ other languages without requiring external translation.
- **Optimization:** **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)** was used to fine-tune the model with minimal VRAM, preserving the original transformer's intelligence while specializing it for "deceptive pattern" detection.

### 2.2 Multimodal Fusion Engine
- **Vision Branch:** Integrated a **ResNet18** feature extractor to analyze images associated with news articles.
- **Adversarial Invariance:** A **Gradient Reversal Layer (GRL)** ensures the model ignores language-specific syntax and focuses on universal indicators of misinformation.
